Switch Character's Gender Information:


You may change your character's gender by paying for the change certain items/amount. All account services changes are final, so please keep that in mind before you apply one of them to a character.  * You may request to change a character's gender as often as you want  * The Gender Change Service is not available for Kamael characters  * When a character's gender is changed, his or her hair style/color and face will be automatically set to type A  * Some gender-specific accessories and items will not be equipable after the Gender Change Service is applied to a character  * You will be disconnected from the server once you click the switch gender button
