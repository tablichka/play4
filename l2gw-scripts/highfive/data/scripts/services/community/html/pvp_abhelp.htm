PvP Buffer Help and Terms of Agreement:


How To Use:
To create your own set of buffs simply enter the name for the set and click the 'Create' button. Then, choose what buffs you'd like to receive and click 'Save' button. Now you may click the 'Buff' or 'Buff servitor' buttons in order to receive appropriate buffs. Also, you may enable "automatic" rebuff if that option is enabled on the server you play.
