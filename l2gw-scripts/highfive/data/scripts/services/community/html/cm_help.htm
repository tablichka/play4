CoL Market Help and Terms of Agreement:


Usage:
To set your items for sale on the CoL Market press the 'Add' button in down right corner of the Community Board. Then choose certain items you wish to sell (each item will cost you adena, as non-refundable fee) and decide on the packace price in Coin of Luck equivalent (minimum equivalent is 2 CoL).
Terms of Agreement:
Please, read carefully the information below before CoL Market usage:  * You will be charged certain amount of adena for each item in the package;  * Each package will be set for sale no longer than days, after which package will be automatically removed from the list;  * In case of successful sale of your package, you will be charged for the service usage, % of the total CoL package price, but no less than CoL;  * You may remove your package at any time, but no adena will be returned to the owner (non-refundable fee);  * You may use search, included in the CoL Market, for free. Also, you may sort columns by price or date, in ascending and descending order;  * Double check prices and the items before you sell or buy in the CoL Market. No rollbacks, returns or refunds will be made by Administration;  * All items and prices set in the CoL Market are defined by players and no administration is involved in this process. All you do on the market is done on your own risks.


     Thanks, and happy trading!
