Priest Gremory:
Then we understand each other. Go to the *Room of Splendor* and *pour water into the Holy Grail.Then pour that water back into a jar and bring it to me*. Thus can we verify the genuineness of the Holy Grail. Be careful, though; the Grail may be guarded. One more thing: the Room of Splendor is locked. You will need a *key to enter there*. Some monks have such keys to perform their duties, so I suggest that you overcome one of them and take his key.
