Gantaki Zu Urutu:
To become a Paagrio Monk, one must have the two scrolls that contain the basic secrets of Kabart, the traditional martial art of the Orcs. The first scroll is called the fiery spirit scroll. To obtain this scroll, you must first seek *Khavatari Rosheek*. [Quest _415_PathToOrcMonk 30587-06.htm|Ask about Khavatari Rosheek]
