[Transfer of Warder]:
[Beginners]

You are now eligible to receive benefits for beginners.
Beginners will receive the following benefits until they reach level 25.
1. Equipment Trade: Used weapons or armor may be traded for better equipment by paying the difference in price between the items. Speak to any armor shop merchant.
2. Support Magic: You can ask the Newbie Guide to cast Support Magic. It can greatly assist you during a battle with monsters. You may receive Support Magic after level 6, with support up to level 62.
3. Additional Supplies of Soulshot and Spiritshot: You will receive an additional reward upon completion of the specific quest. Seek out Zerstorer Marcela at the South Entrance of Kamael Village. He will give you a quest to defeat the Green Goblins, Mountain Werewolf, Mostro Archer, Mountain Werewolf Chief, Mostro Guard and Hill Fungus in the Golden Hills.
Succeed and you will receive Soulshot or Spiritshot for Beginners as a reward.
