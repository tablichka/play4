Ancestor Martankus:
I am Martankus from the Hestui tribe! I have been dead for a thousand years and though my body has disappeared, my soul remains to watch over my descendents! This cave is the holy grave of the heroes of the 7th tribe. It is also where young Orcs come to develop their skills. You have come to learn about essence of Paagrio? The essence cannot be described merely by words. Bring the *Essence of Flame* to perceive the truth! You will find it in the care of the the *lava salamanders* of the *forgotten temple* in the far north of this land.
