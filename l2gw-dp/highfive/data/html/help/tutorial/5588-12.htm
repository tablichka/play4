[player_help help/5588.htm|List] *Newbie Bonus* Once you reach level 6, your character becomes a Newbie and is eligible to receive a number of benefits until reach level 24. Benefits include:
*Equipment Exchange -* At weapons, armor or accessories shops, you will receive the margin when exchanging equipment. All non-grade equipment can be exchanged. *Support Magic -* Guides in villages will cast support magic according to your level (ranges from level 6 to level 62). *Additional Quest Bonuses -* You may receive additional bonus in certain quests.
