[player_help help/5588.htm|List] *Deceased Character* When a character over level 10 dies, he loses a certain amount of experience (EXP). and will suffer a gradual drop in ability. If you have not killed another player, this penalty will not take effect until level 9. If your Karma is 0 (check status window: Alt + T) or your PKs less than 5, you will not drop items if you are killed during PvP. (In a non-PvP server, this does not apply since you cannot attack other characters.) [player_help help/5588.htm|List]
