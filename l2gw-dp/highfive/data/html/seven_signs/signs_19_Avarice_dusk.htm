Dusk Priestess:

[ Seal of Avarice ]
The Seal of Avarice bestows the following benefits:

Ownership of the Necropolises:
The Revolutionaries of Dusk have exclusive rights to the eight Necropolises. In case of a tie, entry is no longer exclusive.
Meeting with Anakim:
The door to the Forbidden Sanctum opens and we finally meet the holy flame, Anakim. She is our greatest enemy, sleeping in the Saint's Necropolis.
Commerce with the Merchant of Mammon:
You can do business with the Merchant of Mammon in the Necropolises. He accepts payment in Ancient Adena, and have many fine, rare articles.
