Guard Upgrade Menu

The Guard Equipment Upgrade level is now %guardpower%.
The Guard Equipment level adds attack and defensive power to your defender units and makes your chances higher in successful fortress defense.
On each of the level you can equip your guards with more powerful weapons and armor. Weapon/Armor higher level cost 100,000 adena. Weapon/Armor *highest* level cost 150,000 adena.
