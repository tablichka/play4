Newbie Guide:
The One-Handed Sword is the most basic weapon in the game, possessing balanced attributes. The Squire's Sword you began your adventures with was one of these. unless you prefer a specific weapon, I recommend staying with a One-Handed Sword. You can also use Fallen Attack with it, which will allow you to strike a critical blow to enemies. [npc_%objectId%_Chat 21|Back.]
