Fishing Manual:
Need some quick fishing tips? here's some useful advive:
When a fish uses a float cheating skill, you can see *a spray of water around the float, and the color of the fish's HP changes.* While the cheating skill is being used, *reeling and pumping should be reversed*. Here's one more piece of advive concerning fluorescent baits. *Fluorescent baits* are strictly for nighttime fishing and are very ineffective *if used during the daytime*.
